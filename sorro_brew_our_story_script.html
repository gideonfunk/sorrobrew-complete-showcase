# Our Story: The Sorro Brew Journey ## Headline: From Salvadoran Volcanic Soil to Your Vancouver Morning **The Spark** Sorro Brew didn't start in a boardroom; it started in the high-altitude hills of El Salvador, in the sun-drenched maicillo fields where Manuel’s family has lived for generations. Growing up, Manuel watched the local *cafetaleros* and farmers toast maicillo (sorghum) as a cherished local ritual—a caffeine-free, nutrient-rich alternative to coffee that didn't sacrifice the deep, roasted soul of a morning cup. **The Mission** When Manuel moved to Vancouver, he brought that ancestral wisdom with him. He saw a city that loved its coffee ritual but struggled with the jitters, acidity, and sleep disruption that came with it. He realized that the "Break up with coffee" movement didn't need a clinical substitute—it needed a heritage-rich, soul-warming evolution. **Our Farmers: The Heart of the Brew** At the core of Sorro Brew is a direct-trade partnership with the very communities Manuel grew up in. We don't just buy grain; we invest in regenerative farming practices that protect the Salvadoran landscape. Every bag of Sorro Brew supports small-scale cooperatives, ensuring fair wages and preserving the traditional toasting methods that give our blend its signature chocolatey, earthy profile. **The Vancouver Wellness Connection** Today, we bridge the gap between ancient Central American tradition and modern West Coast wellness. Sorro Brew is more than a drink; it's a commitment to a better morning, a healthier gut, and a more sustainable world. **Join the Ritual.** *Break up with coffee, not your morning.*
