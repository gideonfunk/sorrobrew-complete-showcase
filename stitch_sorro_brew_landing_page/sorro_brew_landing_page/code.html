Our Roots

From Salvadoran Hills to Your Cup

For generations, Salvadoran farmers have toasted maicillo for its deep, roasted flavor and restorative properties. We’ve brought this ancient wisdom to Vancouver, bridging the gap between ancestral ritual and modern wellness.

Our sorghum is sourced directly from sustainable, small-scale cooperatives in El Salvador, ensuring every harvest supports the land and the hands that tend it.
