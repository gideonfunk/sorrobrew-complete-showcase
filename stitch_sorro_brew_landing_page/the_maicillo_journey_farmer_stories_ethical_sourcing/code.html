Chronicles of the Sun

The Grain of the Sun

Maicillo, the resilient ancient grain of El Salvador, is more than agriculture; it is the pulse of our highlands. Forged in the heat of the tropical sun, it has nourished civilizations for millennia.

Unlike standard grains, Maicillo thrives in the mineral-rich volcanic soil, absorbing the essence of the earth to deliver a flavor profile that is deep, toasted, and remarkably earthy.

The Slow Transformation

01

Thermal Awakening

The raw grains are introduced to seasoned clay ovens at low embers, gently drawing out moisture without bruising the husk.

02

Maillard Mastery

As the temperature rises, the amino acids and sugars within the maicillo react, creating a complex, nut-like profile that mimics the richness of coffee.

03

The Cooling Rest

Post-roast, the grain rests for exactly twelve hours in cool highland air, allowing the oils to settle and the ancestral aroma to peak.
