Ancestral Wisdom: Manuel’s Legacy

In the shadow of the Santa Ana volcano, Manuel’s family perfected the art of the maicillo roast. It wasn't just sustenance; it was a rhythmic connection to the land. They called it 'the seed of the sun.'

Every morning, the aroma of slow-toasted grains filled the air, signaling a day of focus and vitality. We carry this torch forward, using the same slow-roasting techniques passed down through four generations.
